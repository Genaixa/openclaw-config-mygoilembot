## The Simple Explanation

Google is changing. More and more searches now show an AI answer at the top — before any website links. And people are going straight to ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI to ask for recommendations by name.

These AI tools work differently from traditional search. They don't just look at your website. They pull from:
- Google Business Profile
- Bing Places *(this is what ChatGPT uses)*
- Apple Maps *(this is what Siri uses)*
- Reviews across multiple platforms
- Trade directories and citations
- Your website structure and content
- Accreditations (Gas Safe, NICEIC, Checkatrade etc.)

Most local trades businesses have never optimised any of this. Which means they're invisible to AI — even if they rank well on Google.

---

## Why It's Urgent (The Compounding Effect)
This isn't just about being early. Once an AI tool recommends a business, that recommendation gets indexed and referenced by *other* AI tools. Early movers don't just get a head start — they get a growing, compounding head start. The longer a competitor is established in AI search before you, the harder it is to displace them.

## Objection Handlers

**"We already have SEO / a web guy"**
> SEO and GEO are completely different things. Your SEO might be perfect — and you'd still be invisible on ChatGPT. Most SEO agencies aren't doing this yet because it's new. We specialise only in this.

**"We get most of our work from word of mouth"**
> That's the best sign — it means you have a strong reputation. GEO is essentially word of mouth at scale. When AI recommends you, it's passing that reputation on to thousands of people who are actively looking for someone right now.

**"How do I know it's working?"**
> Every month we send you a report with screenshots of real AI searches — "best plumber in Gateshead" — showing exactly where you appear. It's completely transparent and visible.

**"How long before I see results?"**
> Most clients start seeing movement in 4–8 weeks. Full results typically by month 3. And once you're established, it compounds — AI tools reference each other, so your visibility keeps growing.

**"We're too busy right now"**
> Understood. The thing is, this is a first-mover game. Every month you wait is a chance for a competitor in your area to lock in that position before you. Once they're established in AI search, it's much harder to displace them.

**"We'll think about it"**
> Of course — no pressure. Before you go, is there anything you're unsure about that I can clear up now? I'd rather answer it today than have it sit as a question.

**"Is it guaranteed?"**
> No ethical company can guarantee AI results — the AI makes its own decisions. What we guarantee is that we do everything proven to give you the best possible chance, you'll see exactly what's happening every month, and there's no long contract if you're not satisfied.

**"What if we get a bad review?"**
> We handle it. We respond professionally on your behalf — and actually, how a business responds to a bad review is a positive trust signal to AI. A well-handled complaint shows you care. We'll always check with you before responding to anything sensitive.

**"What's the difference between your packages?"**
> All packages include the full service — nothing is cut out, because cutting anything would stop it working. The difference is scale: how many areas we cover, how many review requests we send, how often we update your content. We'd recommend starting based on how many areas you actually work across.
