The Problem

Google is changing. Most local businesses don't know yet.

More and more searches now show an AI answer before any website links. People are going straight to ChatGPT or Google AI and asking by name: "who's a good plumber near me?"

Traditional Search (SEO)

Google ranks your website based on keywords and backlinks. You compete for clicks to your site. Most agencies focus here — and it's getting harder every year as AI takes over the top of the results page.

AI Search (GEO)

AI tools read reviews, directories, structured data, and trusted sources — then recommend businesses by name. It doesn't matter how well you rank on Google. If AI doesn't know you exist, you're invisible to this growing wave of searches.

FAQ

Common questions.

We already have SEO. Is this different?
Yes — completely. SEO targets Google's traditional ranking algorithm. GEO targets AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overview, which use entirely different signals. You can rank number one on Google and still be invisible to AI search. They're separate systems.
How long before we see results?
Most clients start appearing in AI results within 4–8 weeks. Full results are typically visible by month 3. And unlike paid ads, once you're established in AI search, the visibility compounds — it keeps growing over time.
How do I know it's working?
Every month you receive a report with screenshots of real AI searches — "best plumber in [your area]" — showing exactly where and how you appear. It's transparent and concrete.
We get most of our work through word of mouth.
That's a great sign — it means your reputation is strong. GEO is essentially word of mouth at scale. When AI recommends your business, it's passing that reputation on to thousands of people who are actively looking right now.
Is there a contract?
No long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We'd rather keep clients because the results speak for themselves.
What trades do you work with?
We work with any local trades business — plumbers, electricians, builders, roofers, decorators, locksmiths, gardeners, carpenters, and more. If customers search for your service locally, we can help.
